Being secured in everything is key to life and that is what iPhone iOS stands for. From an article found at “COMPUTERWORLD” dated on June 6, 2011, written by Robert Lemos an award- winning freelance journalist, he shared what he termed as “Apple's iOS, why it's the most secure OS, period”. In his remarks, he said “In five major areas, Apple's iOS has better security than desktop operating systems and matches or exceeds the security of its smartphone

  • A sandbox isolates programs, and iOS's memory organization makes exploitation more difficult.

  • Applications that run on the iOS are vetted by Apple and can be removed if found to be malicious.

  • Patches can be quickly applied to the iPhone and iPad to close security holes in the operating system.

  • The software is regularly reviewed, especially its open-source components.

  • The platform has the advantage of attacker psychology -- attackers still target smartphones far less than desktop systems”.

(Source: https://www.computerworld.com/article/2508986/apple-ios--why-it-s-the-most-secure-os--period.html#)
